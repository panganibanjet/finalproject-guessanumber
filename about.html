About the Game

Welcome to Guess a Number from 1 to 100! Are you ready to put your guessing skills to the test?
This classic game is sure to keep you on the edge of your seat as you try to crack the secret number.
Here's how it works: we have a mystery number between 1 and 100, and it's your job to figure out what it is.
You'll make guesses, and we'll provide hints to guide you along the way.
Pay close attention to the hints, as they will help you narrow down your options and get closer to the target.
Remember, the secret number could be any whole number between 1 and 100, so keep your thinking cap on!
Start by making your first guess, and we'll let you know if you're too high or too low.
Use the process of elimination and logical reasoning to make subsequent guesses until you hit the bullseye.
Good luck, and happy guessing!
